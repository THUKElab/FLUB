Introduction

We challenge the reasoning and understanding abilities of LLMs by proposing a FaLlacy Understanding Benchmark (FLUB) containing cunning texts that are easy for humans to understand but difficult for models to grasp. We collect real cunning texts as our raw data from a famous Chinese online forum, the “Ruozhiba” (retard forum). Figure 1a shows the running examples from FLUB.

From these cases, we directly feel the different behaviors of LLMs and humans when facing cunning texts. It is very urgent and meaningful to construct a benchmark composed of cunning texts to evaluate and thereby promote the improvement of LLMs' fallacy understanding capabilities.
